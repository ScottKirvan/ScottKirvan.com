Full comms blackout during a hailstorm. Turned out to be snow load, not the storm itself.

Dish angle plus wet snow equals a physically blocked aperture. Cleared it by hand, signal returned in under a minute. Adding "check for snow load" to the pre-storm checklist, right above "check for mice."
