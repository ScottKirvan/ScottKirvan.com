# [ MISSION BRIEF ]

**Classification:** Citizen Scientist, Unaffiliated

Captain Video operates outside institutional authority — no command structure, no chain of approval. Gomtuu is the mobile command center: self-contained, purposeful, built and maintained by hand.

The mission: document what actually works under field conditions. Not marketing copy, not a highlight reel — the failures too. Equipment isn't proven until it's suffered a kinetic event and kept running.

**Standing orders:**
- Build the tools you need when nobody else will
- Document failures as honestly as successes
- The signal must be sustained
